Metosos

Founder & Developer: Mustafa DOĞAN Active Development Visit metosos.com

About the Project

Metosos is an upcoming AI-powered productivity desktop suite designed to streamline audio transcription, voice execution, and real-time translation. Built with privacy-first engineering standards, Metosos routes queries securely to provide instant, sub-second responses in a clean desktop overlay.

Disclaimer: Metosos is currently under active pre-release development. The platform is undergoing security auditing and GDPR alignment in preparation for its official release.

Key Principles

  • Voice Integration: Designed to capture loops of system audio and microphone feeds to execute commands and transcribe meetings on the go.
  • Real-Time Processing: Integrates fast language models and speech-to-text processing to deliver instantaneous responses.
  • Privacy-First Architecture: Strict data minimization standards process transient queries in-memory. Personal identifiers are decoupled from system logs to adhere to GDPR compliance.
